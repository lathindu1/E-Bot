E-BOT 2017 Chalange

The Electronic socity of Wayamba University of Srilanka (ESOC) proudly presents it's maiden robotic challange E-BOT 2017. The aim of the challange is to be a stepping stone for the amateur robot enthusiasts and to be a platfoarm where they can improve and one day become grate. E-BOT will be held on 11th of November 2017

About Event

E-BOT Games

The challange will be held in two categories, undergraduate event and the Alumini event. The events will be open to current and past students of Wayamba university of Sri Lanka. The objective of having an Alumini category is to provide the amatures a chance to bond with their seniors and to gain invalable experience and technological soundness from them.
